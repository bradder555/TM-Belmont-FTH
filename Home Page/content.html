Welcome to Belmont Toastmasters 

Do you want to be:

  • an engaging public speaker?
  • give better work presentations?
  • participate in meetings more confidently?
  • speak with poise off the cuff?
  • improve the way you handle one-on-one interactions with colleagues, clients, family & friends?

Toastmasters International is a non-profit educational organisation that teaches public speaking and leadership skills through a worldwide network.

At Belmont Toastmasters you join a community of learners where, in a supportive environment, you’ll develop effective communication skills.

                                    

We offer members:

Better speaking skills

Better listening skills

Better leadership skills

Why Belmont Toastmasters?

Enjoys both new and experienced members

Opportunities to practice at each meeting

Progress at your own pace

Receive friendly, valuable coaching

Positive learning environment

Visitors welcome – at no cost
